We help poor children to make their life better.

Agaram which educates the underprivileged 1st generation, rural government school college goers, has grown up to support nearly 2000 children in the past seven years. since vidhai's initiation we have received more than 42000+ applications most of which were deserving, and we have been able to support only 2000 which is only 5% of the need.

For the first time we extend to reach to our supporters in the United States, those who believe and have experienced that education can change life, please Register your details with us, let us meet in person in your city in the United States, learn about Agaram's activities and take this initiative forward of educating as many as deserving children.
